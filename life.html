Growing up, my parents allowed me to experience many artistic extracurriculars (which I am very thankful for). I grew up in an environment where creativity was encouraged and I got to develop and explore my abilities through different mediums. Here are some projects I have worked on as an expression of my mind and interests.

+stories

My background in story writing is a branch off of the Creations block. My mind has always been buzzing with thoughts and ideas and at times while growing up, it became an overwhelming chaos. One method I adopted to better focus that energy was story writing. More often than not, I enjoy writing out the synopsis than a fully fleshed out novel. Overall, I just enjoy bringing the worlds in my head to life on paper.
