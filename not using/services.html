services

Design

Website Design:
Nowadays, there is nothing more important for people and brands to have a professional online presence. One of the key components of a strong online presence is having an eye-catching website. Please email me about your project and any vision you have for your website. We can discuss and plan the best way to give your project the best web presence. Anything from a full website design to a partial website change is possible.

Pre-Design:
There are many steps in designing a website, and oftentimes, site mapping and wireframing are ignored. Building a strong foundation through a well thought out pre-design is crucial to making sure your website has optimal user flow and user experience.

Style Guide:
Having solid and well planned branding is one of the biggest factors to a brand’s success. Without a comprehensive style guide, it is hard to streamline the different aspects of your brand to be stylistically cohesive. For both digital and physical presences, it is important to define the branding style.

Decks:
Decks are a crucial part for many businesses to show a physical representation of their brand. Whether it’s implementing an existing brand concept or developing a new one, we can work together to bring a well thought out deck to life.

Development

Web Design Development:
Once you’ve obtained a desired website design, it is important for good development to take place for the design to come to life. Whether it is my design or from a different designer, we can work together to create a fully functioning website.

Website Elevation:
As you probably know, some websites just feel more luxurious and high quality than others. This is because of small added features that take the website to a new level. Any website can achieve quality elevation through some tweaks and added features.

Bug Fixes:
Any website can have or develop small bugs or broken features. No matter how serious or how insignificant, I can make sure your website runs smoothly once again.

Wordpress:
Wordpress Codex is one of the best platforms to build a website for clients who want to be able to edit and upload content. As an experienced user of Wordpress, I can help you in various ways: implementing a theme, customizing an existing theme, or creating a theme based on a design.
